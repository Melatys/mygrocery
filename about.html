ABOUT US



Our Story

Melatys Speacility Foods was founded in 2023 by a proud Malay Muslim women who had a big dream and an even bigger heart.
With a strong academic background in Computer Science and a full-time job as a software engineer, she started this grocery business as a
passion project - driven by her desire to give back to community while building a halal, trustworthy, and affordable grocery service.



About the Founder

Graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, our founder balances her life between writing code and managing a growing business.
As a tech-savvy woman with deep cultural values, she brings innovation to the grocery industry while maintaining a personal touch rooted in
sincerity and care. Her goal is to prove that modern Muslim womenn can thrive both in tech and entrepreneurship.



Our Mission

To serve our local community with a quality, halal-certified groceries while upholding the values of integrity, affordability,
and excellent customer service.



Our Vision

To become a trusted name in household shopping - one that empowers Muslim women, local suppliers, and provides honest,
value-driven service to families everywhere.
